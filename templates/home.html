HOW IT WORKS:

STEP 1: Uploading your statement/post in word format where you will need to provide your email address, mobile number and the communication will be done through our own developed Chatroom. As the editor might need to ask you questions regarding your submitted post, then you will require to be in contact with the editor.
STEP 2: Your submitted post will be accepted by the admin where he will need to look for which one of the editor has the time to undertake the particular task of editing. The post will be sent to the respective editor.
STEP 3: After the submission of the post, then the editor will get started to edit the paper. The editor will be provided the communication medium of the user where he might need to communicate and ask questions regarding the paper. He will make use of the Chatroom on the website where the user needs to often check his inbox too.
